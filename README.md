# 💸 ExpenseTrackerApp

A modern, intuitive Android application to help you track your daily expenses, manage your budget, and achieve your financial goals effortlessly.

## ✨ Features

- **Dashboard & Analytics:** View your total expenses, balance, and visual summaries of your spending habits.
- **Easy Entry:** Quickly log new transactions with categories, dates, and short notes.
- **Categorization:** Organize expenses with custom or predefined categories (e.g., Food, Transport, Entertainment).
- **Transaction History:** Scroll through a detailed list of all your past activities and filter them by date or type.
- **Budget Management:** Set monthly limits and get notified when you approach your budget ceiling.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Languages:** Kotlin
- **Framework:** Jetpack Compose
- **Gen AI:** Gemini API
- **Build System:** Gradle
- **Platform:** Android Framework
- **Architecture:** Clean Architecture with MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
